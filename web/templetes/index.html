Brief Instructions

Your task as a bait is to attract the fortress' attention by entering the inner hexagon where it is vulnerable to the fortress. The fortress becomes vulnerable when attempting to shoot at you, which gives your AI partner a chance to destroy it from behind.

You will gain 100 scores every time your team successes in destroying the fortress, and lose 100 scores every time one of the player dies. In other words, your performance is measured by the team performance. Please be adaptive and collaborate with you partner to maximum them team performance.

D or right arrow - clockwise rotation , A or left arrow - counterclockwise rotation, J of F - shoot, W or up arrow - thrust
